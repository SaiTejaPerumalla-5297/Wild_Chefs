By: Meera from Chennai, India
Cuisine: South Indian

"This tangy and comforting dish reminds me of summer lunches at home."

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 6‚Äì8 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
  2. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and turmeric.
  3. Add rice, lemon juice, and salt. Mix gently.
  4. Top with roasted peanuts and serve warm.
