  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)

  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps:

By: Min-Ji from Seoul, South Korea
Cuisine: Korean

"Perfect way to use leftover rice ‚Äî spicy, quick, and satisfying!"

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi
  • 1 tbsp gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Spring onions for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan.
  2. Add kimchi and gochujang, cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in rice and soy sauce. Mix well.
  4. (Optional) Top with a fried egg. Garnish with spring onions.
